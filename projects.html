Project 1: Building Regulations Navigator

Problem Statement: How might we help architects and urban planners navigate complex and often ambiguous building regulations when current methods require digging through dense, scattered documents, leading to wasted time, misinterpretations, costly redesigns, delayed approvals, and missed compliance risks that can derail entire projects?

Solution: A prototype website designed to streamline the process of navigating building regulations, providing a centralized and user-friendly platform for architects and urban planners.
